Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about sourcing, preparation, shipping, and the preview storefront.

What does wildcrafted mean?

Wildcrafted plants are gathered from their natural habitat. Responsible harvest limits and local knowledge matter.

Are your teas in tea bags?

No. Kawsaypac products use loose whole botanicals in resealable pouches.

How do I choose a blend?

Begin with Shop by Concern, read the product context, and ask a qualified professional about personal risks.

Is checkout active?

No. Product buttons demonstrate the intended experience. Shopify checkout is connected after client approval.

Do herbs replace medical care?

No. Products and education are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease.
