The Stay

Stay in Sacred Luxury

Your surroundings are part of the medicine. Every detail — from where you sleep to how you move between landscapes — is curated for depth, comfort, and beauty.

Highlands Retreat

Sacred Andes Garden Oasis

Wake up nestled inside a garden oasis in one of the highest cities in the world. Quito's historic center sits at 9,350 feet in the Andes — where the air is crisp, the light is golden, and the city's colonial beauty surrounds you. Your base here is lush, grounded, and unlike anything you've experienced.

Baños de Agua Santa

Pandora — River Valley Sanctuary

This is Pandora. Perched above a rushing stream in a valley draped in mist and surrounded by mountains on every side, Baños de Agua Santa is one of Ecuador's most breathtaking landscapes. Waterfalls cascade through the hills. The air is warm, alive, electric. You'll fall asleep to the sound of the river and wake to a world that looks like it was imagined, not discovered.

Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Have a question not answered here? Your discovery call is the place to ask everything.

A discovery call is a private 30-minute conversation with our team. We'll learn about where you are, what you're carrying, and what you're hoping to experience — and share exactly how the retreat works. There's no pressure. It's about finding out if this is the right fit, together.
No prior experience is required. Many participants come with open hearts and no ceremony background — and have the most profound experiences of their lives. Our facilitators are experienced at holding space for people at all levels. You'll be fully supported, prepared, and informed at every step.
Each day is a carefully curated journey. Mornings typically begin with breathwork, movement, or meditation. Days include healing modalities, workshops, ceremonial experiences, nature immersion, and communal plant-based meals. Evenings are intentional — often ceremony, integration circles, or quiet reflection. No two days are the same.
The investment covers all accommodations (both Andes and Amazon stays), all plant-based meals, all ceremonial and healing experiences, in-country transportation between locations, and full facilitation and support throughout the 10 days. International flights are not included. Full pricing details are shared on your discovery call.
We provide a pre-retreat preparation guide that outlines nutritional, lifestyle, and mindset recommendations in the weeks leading up to arrival. Guided by The Electric Eats philosophy, preparation is gentle and supportive — designed to help your body and nervous system arrive as open and receptive as possible.
We work exclusively in vetted, safe, and sacred locations. The retreat sites in both the Andes and the Amazon are private, secure, and have been chosen for their beauty, safety, and spiritual significance. Our team handles all logistics, and you will be accompanied at every point of your journey.
This is a small-group experience — deliberately. We strictly limit group size to ensure every participant receives personal attention, genuine integration support, and a truly intimate experience. Once the cohort is full, doors close. If you feel called, don't wait.
Transformation doesn't end when you land home — it continues. Post-retreat integration is a core part of our philosophy. You'll leave with a personalized integration framework, access to our community, and follow-up support to help you anchor the shifts you've made into your everyday life.
