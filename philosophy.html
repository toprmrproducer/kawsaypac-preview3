Why Kawsaypac

Our Philosophy

Plant knowledge should bring you closer to your body, your source, and the living world.

Relationship before extraction

Kawsaypac begins with direct relationships across Ecuador. We work with people who know the land, the harvest seasons, and the limits of what each ecosystem can give.

Wildcrafted is a responsibility to the plant, the partner, and the person preparing the cup.

Whole plants, clear context

We favor small batches and whole botanicals because customers deserve to see what they are using. Preparation notes, origin context, and responsible language are part of the product.

Six standards

  • Ecuador sourced through direct relationships
  • Small-batch production
  • Wildcrafted whole botanicals
  • Research-informed education
  • Clear preparation guidance
  • Ethical stewardship
