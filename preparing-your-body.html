Shop the collection Preparation guide

A useful herbal ritual does not need to begin with intensity. It begins with attention.

Make space before adding more

Start with regular meals, water, rest, and an honest picture of what your body already manages. A new pouch is not a rescue plan; it is an addition to a foundation that already holds. Give it one, and it will give you clearer answers.

The method

Begin one change at a time.

01

Read the whole label

The full label and ingredient list, before the first cup. Know exactly what you are inviting in.

02

Choose a consistent time

Same hour, same place. A ritual anchored to your day tells you more than one that drifts.

03

Keep honest notes

How you prepared it, how you felt after. Two lines a day is enough to see a pattern.

04

Never stack new things

Avoid starting several new products at once. One variable at a time, or the signal disappears.

05

Pause when unsure

If something feels wrong, stop and seek guidance. Pausing is part of the practice, not a failure of it.

Consistency gives you clearer information than intensity.
