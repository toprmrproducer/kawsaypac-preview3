Preparing Your Body

Build a calmer foundation before adding a new herbal ritual.

Make space before adding more

A useful herbal ritual does not need to begin with intensity. Start with regular meals, water, rest, and an honest picture of what your body already manages.

Begin one change at a time

  • Read the full label and ingredient list
  • Choose a consistent time of day
  • Keep notes on preparation and response
  • Avoid stacking several new products at once
  • Pause and seek guidance if something feels wrong
Consistency gives you clearer information than intensity.

When to ask for help

Speak with a qualified healthcare professional if you take medication, have a diagnosed condition, are pregnant or nursing, or plan to use herbs with children.
